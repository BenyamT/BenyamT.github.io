ABOUT TL

WHO WE ARE

Established in 2016, TL Foundation Specialist has emerged as o n e of the best in the industry in providing sustainable construction solutions. We are dedicated to delivering projects on time, specializing in shoring and piling services. Our expertise includes designing and implementing foundation and shoring systems for a variety of structures, including high rise buildings, factories, bridges, and more.
Our machinery and tools reflect our commitment to delivering top tier services that meet the highest industry standards. At TL Foundation Specialist, our dedication to excellence and innovation positions us among the elite companies in the region capable of providing high-quality,specialized foundation solutions. Our team consists of highly experienced foundation and piling professionals who bring extensive knowledge and hands on expertise to every project. Having actively contributed to numerous projects in Ethiopia and East Africa, our team has played a pivotal role in the region’s adoption and advancement of modern foundation technologies,initially introduced by the renowned German specialist,Bauer.

OUR MISSION and VISION

MISSION

At TL Foundation Specialist, our mission is to advance the field of foundation and shoring by integrating cutting edge technology and delivering customized engineering solutions. We are dedicated to developing a skilled work force and establishing best practices in shoring works. Our commitment extends to promoting industry standards and fostering collaboration among stake holders to drive progress and efficiency in construction.
